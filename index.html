About me

Welcome to my website! You will find here information about my work, my research, and collaborations. I am the founder of Owls & Arrows, a consultancy company focused on responsible AI and algorithmic auditing. I help clients in the private and public sectors adopt best practices to ensure responsible use of AI-driven systems, and conduct impact assessments exercises. I also conduct research with academic and industry collaborators, I am particularly interested in developing practical solutions for explainable, fair machine learning. You can read more about my research activities on the R&D page.
